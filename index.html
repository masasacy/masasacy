[Ramadhan]
Dua after breaking our fast

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ

"Thirst is gone, the veins are moistened and the reward is certain if Allah wills"

(English translation)

"Rasa haus telah hilang dan urat-urat telah basah, dan pahala telah ditetapkan insya Allah"

(Indonesian translation)

We say that du'a after breaking our fast.

Here are the details of what we need to do:

  1. Say "Bismillah"
  2. Break our fast
  3. Then say:

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ
